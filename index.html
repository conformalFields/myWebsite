Hello fellow being! I am currently in my 6th year of PhD program studying mathematics at Denver University. For better or worse, my interests are largely unconstrained. I most enjoy mathematics, physics, computer science, economics, and philosophy. I share similar passion for arts, and in a quiet moments I enjoy making a painting or a drawing, and perhaps writing a bad poem. Martial arts is another art-form that I love, jiu-jitsu in particular!

Research

As of 2022, I have two main research interests - field theory(CFT) and machine learning(ML). In short, I am interested in representation theory, infinite-dimensional Lie algebras, and self-supervised learning, modeling evolution and games.
